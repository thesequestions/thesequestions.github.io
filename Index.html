ABOUT STEVE

Steve apprenticed as a carpenter a lifetime ago and has worked on projects big and small over the years but has specialised in woodturning.

He uses found and recycled wood to create items based on the feel of the wood.

"I have to work with the wood to get a feel for it which shapes how it will turn out."

Woodworker: Steve P

Services: Woodturning - decorations, pots, vases, bowls made to order.

Materials: Hard wood, soft wood, reclaimed wood, resin.

Timescale: Commissions are received, wood sourced, prepared and finished ready for postage in 10 working days.

WHAT WE MAKE

All items are custom made and the final look would depend upon the wood used - e.g. polished or with new wood which will split and age for a more rustic look. Call for a chat or message and let us know what kind of thing you would be interested in.
